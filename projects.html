Community Projects

We are focused on developing community partnerships where we learn about the issues individuals are facing and work with community leaders to determine an adequate solution to the issue.

We provide meaningful ways for college students to get involved within North Carolina communities.

By asking the question "How Can We Help?", Thrive Together members apply their own skills and interests to better those around them and create positive change, specifically for young children and adolescents.

Our Process:

1

We will first identify communities with urgent needs. Thrive Together focuses on supporting adolescent populations and resolving structural disparities that affect later-in-life outcomes.

2

We will then collaborate with the community and with local, university, state, and governmental organizations to obtain resources and funding to support our project.

3

We will finally work with the community to design and implement a sustainable solution. To monitor long term success, we will rely on community feedback over time.
